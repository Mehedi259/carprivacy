1. Introduction & Scope

Welcome to Auto Intel. This Privacy Policy explains how Auto Intel ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Key Points:

  • We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We collect only the data necessary to provide and improve our services.
  • You have full control over your data and can request deletion at any time.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure storage, and regular security audits.

This policy applies to all users of the Auto Intel mobile application, available on iOS (Apple App Store) and Android (Google Play Store). By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Scope of Application:

  • Auto Intel mobile application (iOS and Android)
  • All features and services within the App
  • Communications related to the App

2. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our services. Below is a complete list of data types we collect, organized by category:

2.1 Account Information

Data Collected:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Account credentials (encrypted password)

Purpose: Account creation, authentication, user communication, service notifications

Collection Method: Directly provided by you during registration

Retention Period: Until you request account deletion

Linked to Identity: Yes

2.2 Vehicle Information

Data Collected:

  • Vehicle make, model, and year
  • Vehicle Identification Number (VIN)
  • Current mileage
  • License plate number (optional)
  • Vehicle color and trim

Purpose: Personalized maintenance recommendations, service scheduling, parts compatibility, warranty tracking

Collection Method: Directly provided by you when adding vehicles

Retention Period: Tied to your account lifecycle; deleted when you remove the vehicle or delete your account

Linked to Identity: Yes

2.3 Photos and Images

Data Collected:

  • Vehicle photos
  • Issue documentation images (damage, wear, dashboard warnings)
  • Service receipt images

Purpose: AI-based visual diagnosis, maintenance records, issue tracking, evidence for warranty claims

Collection Method: Captured via device camera or selected from device gallery using device-native image picker

Storage: Stored locally on device with optional encrypted cloud backup

Retention Period: User-controlled; you can delete images at any time

Linked to Identity: Yes

Third-Party Access: Images may be processed by our AI analysis service (encrypted during transmission)

2.4 Documents and Files

Data Collected:

  • Service records (PDF, images)
  • Maintenance receipts
  • Warranty documents
  • Insurance papers

Purpose: Service history tracking, warranty management, expense tracking, maintenance scheduling

Collection Method: Uploaded using device file picker

Storage: Secure encrypted file system

Retention Period: User-controlled; retained until you delete them

Linked to Identity: Yes

2.5 Usage Data and Analytics

Data Collected:

  • Features and screens accessed
  • Button clicks and user interactions
  • Time spent in app
  • Search queries within the app
  • Feature usage patterns

Purpose: App improvement, feature development, bug identification, user experience optimization

Collection Method: Automatically collected during app usage

Anonymization: Data is aggregated and anonymized where possible

Retention Period: 24 months

Linked to Identity: May be linked for troubleshooting; otherwise aggregated

2.6 Device Information and Identifiers

Data Collected:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version (iOS/Android)
  • App version
  • Device unique identifier (anonymized)
  • Screen resolution
  • Language and region settings

Purpose: App compatibility, crash reporting, technical support, performance optimization

Collection Method: Automatically collected when you use the App

Retention Period: 12 months

Linked to Identity: No (used for technical purposes only)

2.7 Diagnostics and Crash Data

Data Collected:

  • Crash logs and error reports
  • Performance data (load times, response times)
  • Memory and battery usage
  • Network connectivity information

Purpose: Bug fixing, stability improvements, performance optimization

Collection Method: Automatically collected when errors occur

Retention Period: 90 days

Linked to Identity: No

2.8 Notification Data

Data Collected:

  • Push notification tokens
  • Notification preferences
  • Notification interaction data (opened, dismissed)

Purpose: Sending maintenance reminders, service alerts, app updates, important notifications

Collection Method: Generated by device operating system when you enable notifications

User Control: You can disable notifications in device settings at any time

Retention Period: Until you disable notifications or delete your account

Linked to Identity: Yes

2.9 Local Storage Data

Data Collected:

  • App preferences and settings
  • Cached vehicle data
  • Offline content
  • Search history

Purpose: Offline functionality, faster app performance, seamless user experience

Storage Location: Stored locally on your device only (not transmitted to servers)

Retention Period: Until app is uninstalled or cache is cleared

Linked to Identity: No

2.10 Location Data

Data Collected: None

Note: Auto Intel does NOT collect, store, or share your precise or approximate location data. If future versions include location features (e.g., finding nearby service centers), we will update this policy and request your explicit permission.

2.11 Financial Information

Data Collected: None directly

Note: We do not collect or store payment card information. If the App includes in-app purchases, all payment processing is handled securely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We only receive confirmation of successful transactions.

8. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure storage, and regular security audits.

8.1 Technical Security

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS 1.3 encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Sensitive data stored on servers is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with individual salts
  • Secure APIs: All API endpoints require authentication and use secure protocols
  • Regular Updates: Security patches applied promptly

8.2 Operational Security

  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access to user data
  • Employee Training: Staff trained on data protection and security best practices
  • Security Audits: Regular third-party security assessments
  • Incident Response: Documented procedures for security incidents
  • Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for suspicious activity

8.3 Device-Level Security

  • Local Storage Protection: App data protected by device-level encryption (iOS Keychain, Android Keystore)
  • Biometric Authentication: Optional Face ID/Touch ID/Fingerprint support
  • Secure File Storage: Files stored in app-specific secure directories

Your Responsibility

While we implement strong security measures, please help protect your account by:

  • Using a strong, unique password
  • Enabling device security (passcode, biometrics)
  • Not sharing your login credentials
  • Logging out on shared devices
  • Keeping your app updated
  • Reporting suspicious activity immediately

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours (as required by GDPR)
  • Inform relevant regulatory authorities
  • Provide details about the breach and affected data
  • Outline steps taken to mitigate harm
  • Recommend actions you should take

13. GDPR Rights (European Union Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

13.1 Right to Access (Article 15)

You have the right to obtain:

  • Confirmation of whether we process your personal data
  • A copy of your personal data
  • Information about how we use your data

How to Exercise: Email privacy@autointel.com or use in-app data export feature

13.2 Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You have the right to have inaccurate personal data corrected and incomplete data completed.

How to Exercise: Update in app settings or contact privacy@autointel.com

13.3 Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten" (Article 17)

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data when:

  • Data is no longer necessary for its original purpose
  • You withdraw consent and there's no other legal basis
  • You object to processing and there are no overriding legitimate grounds
  • Data was unlawfully processed
  • Deletion is required for legal compliance

How to Exercise: Use in-app account deletion or email privacy@autointel.com

13.4 Right to Restriction of Processing (Article 18)

You can request that we limit processing of your data when:

  • You contest the accuracy of the data
  • Processing is unlawful but you don't want data deleted
  • We no longer need the data but you need it for legal claims
  • You've objected to processing pending verification

How to Exercise: Email privacy@autointel.com

13.5 Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

You have the right to:

  • Receive your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format
  • Transmit your data to another controller

How to Exercise: Use in-app data export feature (JSON format) or email privacy@autointel.com

13.6 Right to Object (Article 21)

You have the right to object to:

  • Processing based on legitimate interests
  • Direct marketing (including profiling)
  • Processing for scientific/historical research or statistics

How to Exercise: Email privacy@autointel.com with specific objection

13.7 Right Not to Be Subject to Automated Decision-Making (Article 22)

You have the right not to be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing, including profiling, that produces legal effects or similarly significantly affects you.

Our Practice: We do not make automated decisions that significantly affect you without human review.

13.8 Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7)

Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

How to Exercise: In app settings or email privacy@autointel.com

13.9 Right to Lodge a Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, particularly in the EU member state of:

  • Your habitual residence
  • Your place of work
  • The place of the alleged infringement

EU Supervisory Authorities: Find your local authority at edpb.europa.eu

Response Timeline

We will respond to your GDPR requests within 30 days. If your request is complex, we may extend this by an additional 60 days and will notify you of the extension.

Verification

To protect your privacy, we may need to verify your identity before responding to requests. We may ask for:

  • Account email confirmation
  • Security questions
  • Government-issued ID (for sensitive requests)

How to Exercise: Update in app settings or email privacy@autointel.com
